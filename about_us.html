Our Company Overview

Want to Know About Us?

AMESPROJECT is a company dedicated towards sustainable agriculture, recycling of all kinds of waste, as well as promoting sustainable environment. We are working very hard towards becoming the leading force in the agriculture and recycling industry by leveraging a well thought out business plan executed by our skilled management team. One of the areas of our business will be to collect, recycle, compost and and market waste for municipalities for use as consumer goods. And the recycled product will meet two critical needs:

1. It will help meet the ever growing demand for organic soil enhancers and fertilizers. Most of the materials that will be recycled are human waste sludge

2. It will give municipalities a feasible and cost effective alternative to land filling the waste.

Our recycled waste will be targeted towards fertilizer manufacturers, nurseries, landscapers, farmers, government agencies, golf courses, etc. All these potential customers will benefit from the composts numerous soil enhancing characteristics.

Furthermore, we will offer a service to waste processing plants owned by the government. For instance: in lagos, the waste processing plants faces several issues regarding the disposal of human waste sludges. AMESPROJECT will solved this problem by accepting this waste at a nominial charge and recycling it into a useful product to achieve waste to wealth.

Our Mission

AMESPROJECT mission is threefold. Our first responsibility is to ensure the financial well being of the business. Secondly, is to provide municipalities with an economical alternative for the desposal of human waste sludge, industrial waste, construction and demolition generated waste, agricultural waste, as well as commercial and institutional wastes. Thirdly, is to provide a top quality recycled material to the consumer so that they may benefit from composts many good properties and organic elements. In addition, we hope to build in the customer a positive feeling about the feasibility of using recycled human sludge as fertilizer, and possibilities for a healthy environment, economic equality, as well as sustainable future.

Our vision

AMESPROJECT is venturing into a niche market in that human waste sludge and tire recycling that has not been recycled on any sizeable scale in Nigeria or any of the west African countries. This is a unique and viable concept that addresses the needs of various customers and reaches an untapped market with tremendous growth potential.
